# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Solo developer — the tool's builder is its only user. Used at a desk, focused session, building a B2B lead pipeline. Context is deliberate work: defining a geographic area, running a scrape, reviewing leads, composing outreach.

## Product Purpose

A personal B2B lead generation pipeline built on Google Maps data. The user draws a map polygon, kicks off a scrape across a grid of cells, explores the resulting business database, and fires AI-assisted cold emails to qualified leads. Success looks like: a batch of targeted, personalized outreach emails sent in under 10 minutes per session.

## Brand Personality

Premium, warm, focused.

The tool should feel like a precision instrument that has been cared for — not a dashboard demo, not a dev terminal. Warmth comes from typographic rhythm, intentional spacing, and the teal accent reading as a considered choice rather than a default. Focus means nothing competes: each view has one primary action, everything else supports it.

## Anti-references

- **Notion / Linear** — white background, editorial whitespace, large display text. Too calm, too SaaS.
- **HubSpot / Salesforce** — enterprise CRM weight: heavy chrome, badge-heavy status columns, corporate blue palettes.
- **Vercel / Railway dashboards** — terminal-native dark mode: monospace-everywhere, stark, deliberately spare. The right register but stripped of warmth.
- **Generic AI SaaS (2024–2025)** — purple or violet gradients, cream or near-white backgrounds, glassy cards, ubiquitous uppercase eyebrow labels. Saturated AI aesthetic; instantly dated.

## Design Principles

1. **Data is the product.** Business names, counts, enriched signals, email drafts — these are what matter. UI frames them and gets out of the way.
2. **Precision over decoration.** Every element earns its place by serving a workflow step. Nothing is there to look good in isolation.
3. **Warmth through restraint.** "Premium and warm" is not achieved by adding warmth-colored surfaces — it comes from type rhythm, spacing, and an accent that reads as a deliberate choice. The dark palette is already committed; lean into it.
4. **Quiet confidence.** The tool knows what it is. No overselling copy, no onboarding tours, no congratulatory states. Feedback is immediate and factual.
5. **Control feels immediate.** Every user action — drawing a polygon, starting a scrape, sending an email — must produce instant visible feedback. Latency is masked with progress, never silence.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Full WCAG AA: body text contrast ≥ 4.5:1, large text ≥ 3:1, keyboard navigation functional on all interactive elements, visible focus states, reduced-motion alternatives for all animations.
